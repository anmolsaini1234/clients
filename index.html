Fix your apple device
Established in 2008, iRepair India is the country’s most experienced service center for fixing Apple devices. We provide hardware and software support for iPhones, iPods, iPads, Apple Watches and Macs.

iPhone, iPod, iPad and Macbook Repair & Screen Replacement Services

Shattered your iPhone screen? We know – that’s almost as painful as a broken heart. Get your iPhone screen replacement in 60 minutes.
Battery not as efficient as it used to be? Broke your iPad Screen before your presentation? Drop by one of our stores and get your iPad fixed in 2 hours.
Sync issues stopping you from listening to your new favorite song? We’ll help your iPod get back in tune with your life as quickly as possible.
Dropped hot coffee on the keyboard? Ouch. Give us 24 hours and we’ll get your MacBook Pro up and running like new.
Unable to power on your Apple Watch? Scratched the face of the device? We’ll identify and fix the problem in no time.
We can help you maximize the longevity and productivity of your organization’s Apple devices.

100% transparency

We solemnly swear to always tell you why your device is up to no good. No vague problem definitions – we’ll tell you the exact issue, and if applicable, which part is faulty before we proceed to fixing the problem. We’ll also give you the approximate costs involved and time required to resolve the problem.

Quality Parts

We’re proud of the relationship we’ve built with our suppliers over the years. This has enabled us to source high-quality and genuine spares or replacement parts, allowing us to give you the best after sales support and service experience for Apple devices. We are also ISO 9001:2008 compliant, ensuring that all parts and processes used meet relevant statutory and regulatory requirements.

Expert Technicians

We’ve been in business for 9+ years, and have serviced 20,000+ customers across the country. Our team of trained technicians ensure that you get not just the fastest and most efficient but also long-lasting resolution to an issue across all five iRepair India centers.
